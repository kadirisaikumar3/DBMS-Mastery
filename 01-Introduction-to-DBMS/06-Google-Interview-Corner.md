# 🎯 Google Interview Corner

## Must Know Concepts

### What is DBMS?

Most asked DBMS interview question.

---

### Why DBMS over File System?

Google frequently asks:

"Why not simply store data in files?"

Expected Answer:

- Reduced redundancy
- Better security
- Concurrency control
- Data consistency

---

### Advantages of DBMS

- Data Independence
- Security
- Integrity
- Backup & Recovery
- Concurrent Access

---

### Real Interview Tip

Never define DBMS in one line.

Explain:

1. What it is
2. Why it is needed
3. Examples
4. Real-world usage

This creates a stronger interview answer.
