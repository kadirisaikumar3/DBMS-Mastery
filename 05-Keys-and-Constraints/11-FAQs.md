# 💬 FAQs

### What is a Key?

Attribute used to identify records.

---

### What is Primary Key?

Unique identifier of a record.

---

### Can a table have multiple Candidate Keys?

Yes.

---

### Can a table have multiple Primary Keys?

No.

---

### What is Foreign Key?

Attribute referencing another table.

---

### Why are Constraints important?

They ensure data integrity.
