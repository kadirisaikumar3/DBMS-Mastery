# 🎯 Google Interview Corner

## Most Asked Concepts

### Primary Key

Properties:

- Unique
- Not NULL

---

### Candidate Key

Can become Primary Key.

Example:

StudentID

Email

---

### Foreign Key

Creates relationships between tables.

---

### Composite Key

Combination of multiple columns.

Example:

(StudentID, CourseID)

---

### Constraints

Most Frequently Asked:

- NOT NULL
- UNIQUE
- CHECK
- DEFAULT
- FOREIGN KEY

---

## Interview Tip

Remember:

Every Primary Key is a Candidate Key.

But every Candidate Key is not a Primary Key.
