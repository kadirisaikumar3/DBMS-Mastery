# ❌ Common Mistakes

## Mistake 1

Thinking 1NF means no duplicate rows.

Wrong.

1NF requires atomic values.

---

## Mistake 2

Confusing Partial and Transitive Dependency.

2NF → Partial Dependency

3NF → Transitive Dependency

---

## Mistake 3

Assuming BCNF and 3NF are identical.

BCNF is stricter.

---

## Mistake 4

Over-normalizing databases.

Can impact performance.
