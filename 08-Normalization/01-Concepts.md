# 📖 Normalization Concepts

## What is Normalization?

Normalization is a database design technique used to reduce data redundancy and improve consistency.

---

## Why Normalization?

Benefits:

- Reduces redundancy
- Improves consistency
- Saves storage
- Prevents anomalies
- Improves data integrity

---

## Types of Anomalies

1. Insertion Anomaly
2. Update Anomaly
3. Deletion Anomaly

---

## Normal Forms

1NF → First Normal Form

2NF → Second Normal Form

3NF → Third Normal Form

BCNF → Boyce-Codd Normal Form

---

## Goal

Organize data efficiently with minimal duplication.
