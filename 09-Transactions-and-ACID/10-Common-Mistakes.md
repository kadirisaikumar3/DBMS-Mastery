# ❌ Common Mistakes

## Mistake 1

Confusing Atomicity and Consistency.

Atomicity:

All Operations Execute

Consistency:

Valid Database State

---

## Mistake 2

Thinking COMMIT can be undone.

After COMMIT:

Changes become permanent.

---

## Mistake 3

Ignoring Isolation.

Isolation prevents concurrent transaction issues.

---

## Mistake 4

Memorizing ACID without examples.

Always explain with Banking Transactions.
