# 🔑 Key Concepts

## ACID Properties

ACID stands for:

- Atomicity
- Consistency
- Isolation
- Durability

---

## Atomicity

All operations execute completely or none execute.

Example:

Money Transfer

Both debit and credit occur together.

---

## Consistency

Database remains valid before and after transaction.

---

## Isolation

Transactions do not interfere with each other.

---

## Durability

Committed data survives system crashes.

---

## Commit

Permanently saves changes.

---

## Rollback

Undoes changes.
