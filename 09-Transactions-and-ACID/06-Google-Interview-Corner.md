# 🎯 Google Interview Corner

## Most Asked Concepts

### ACID Properties

Every DBMS interview asks this.

ACID =

A → Atomicity

C → Consistency

I → Isolation

D → Durability

---

### Atomicity

All operations happen together.

Example:

Money Transfer

Debit + Credit

Both succeed or both fail.

---

### Isolation

Concurrent transactions should not affect each other.

---

### Durability

Committed data survives power failure.

---

## Interview Tip

Always explain ACID using a Banking Example.

Interviewers love practical examples.
