# ❌ Common Mistakes

## Mistake 1

Using HAVING instead of WHERE.

WHERE filters rows.

HAVING filters groups.

---

## Mistake 2

Forgetting ORDER BY direction.

ASC = Ascending

DESC = Descending

---

## Mistake 3

Using GROUP BY incorrectly.

All non-aggregated columns must appear in GROUP BY.

---

## Mistake 4

Confusing DISTINCT and GROUP BY.

DISTINCT removes duplicates.

GROUP BY creates groups.
