# 📖 DBMS Interview Preparation

## Why Interview Questions?

Interview preparation is different from learning theory.

Companies expect candidates to:

- Explain concepts clearly
- Compare technologies
- Solve scenarios
- Optimize SQL queries
- Design scalable databases

---

## Interview Categories

1. DBMS Basics
2. SQL
3. Transactions
4. Indexes
5. Normalization
6. Concurrency
7. NoSQL
8. System Design

---

## Interview Strategy

- Understand Concepts
- Practice SQL
- Revise Frequently
- Explain with Examples
