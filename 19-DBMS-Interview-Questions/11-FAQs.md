# 💬 FAQs

### How many DBMS questions are enough for interviews?

100–150 high-quality questions cover most SDE-1 interviews.

---

### Is SQL more important than theory?

Both are equally important.

---

### Should I learn NoSQL?

Yes. Modern companies frequently use MongoDB, Redis, and Cassandra.

---

### Are System Design concepts needed for freshers?

Basic database design and scaling concepts are helpful and often discussed.

---

### How often should I revise DBMS?

At least once a week before interviews.
