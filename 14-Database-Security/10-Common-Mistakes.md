# ❌ Common Mistakes

## Mistake 1

Confusing Authentication and Authorization.

Authentication:

Identity

Authorization:

Permissions

---

## Mistake 2

Storing passwords in plain text.

Always hash passwords.

---

## Mistake 3

Ignoring SQL Injection prevention.

Use parameterized queries.

---

## Mistake 4

Not taking regular backups.

Can result in permanent data loss.

---

## Mistake 5

Giving excessive permissions to users.

Follow Principle of Least Privilege.
