# 💬 FAQs

### What is Concurrency Control?

Managing simultaneous transactions safely.

---

### Why is it important?

Maintains consistency and integrity.

---

### What is Lost Update?

One update overwrites another.

---

### What is Dirty Read?

Reading uncommitted data.

---

### What is Serializability?

Concurrent execution behaves like serial execution.

---

### What is 2PL?

Two-Phase Locking protocol.
