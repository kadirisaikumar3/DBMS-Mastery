# ❌ Common Mistakes

## Mistake 1

Confusing Dirty Read and Lost Update.

Dirty Read:

Read Uncommitted Data.

Lost Update:

Update Overwritten.

---

## Mistake 2

Thinking Shared Lock allows writing.

Wrong.

Shared Lock only allows reading.

---

## Mistake 3

Ignoring Deadlocks.

Deadlocks are a major concurrency issue.

---

## Mistake 4

Not understanding Serializability.

Serializability ensures correctness.
