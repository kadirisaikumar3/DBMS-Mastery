# ❌ Common Mistakes

## Mistake 1

Thinking NoSQL means "No SQL."

NoSQL means "Not Only SQL."

---

## Mistake 2

Using NoSQL for every application.

Relational databases are still better for highly structured transactional systems.

---

## Mistake 3

Confusing CAP Theorem with ACID.

CAP applies to distributed systems.

ACID applies to transactions.

---

## Mistake 4

Assuming NoSQL databases do not support transactions.

Many modern NoSQL databases provide transaction support.

---

## Mistake 5

Choosing a database without considering access patterns and scalability requirements.
