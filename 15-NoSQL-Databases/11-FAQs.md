# 💬 FAQs

### What is NoSQL?

A non-relational database designed for scalability and flexibility.

---

### Is MongoDB a NoSQL database?

Yes.

---

### What is Redis?

A Key-Value NoSQL database used mainly for caching.

---

### What is Cassandra?

A distributed Column-Family database.

---

### What is Neo4j?

A Graph Database.

---

### What is CAP Theorem?

A distributed system can guarantee only two among Consistency, Availability, and Partition Tolerance.
