# 💬 FAQs

### What is Database Scaling?

Increasing database capacity to handle more traffic.

---

### What is Sharding?

Splitting data across multiple servers.

---

### What is Replication?

Maintaining copies of data across servers.

---

### What is Redis?

An in-memory database commonly used for caching.

---

### Why are Read Replicas used?

To reduce load on the primary database.

---

### What is High Availability?

Keeping the system operational even during failures.
