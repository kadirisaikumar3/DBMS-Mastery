# 🎯 Google Interview Corner

## Frequently Asked Topics

### Relation

Interviewers often ask:

"What is a Relation?"

Answer:

A table in a relational database.

---

### Tuple

A row in a relation.

Example:

(1, Sai, 23)

---

### Degree

Number of columns.

Example:

Student(ID, Name, Age)

Degree = 3

---

### Cardinality

Number of rows.

Example:

100 rows

Cardinality = 100

---

## Interview Tip

Remember:

Degree = Columns

Cardinality = Rows

This is a very common interview question.
