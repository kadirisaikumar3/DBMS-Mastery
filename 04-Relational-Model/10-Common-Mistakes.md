# ❌ Common Mistakes

## Mistake 1

Confusing Tuple and Attribute.

Tuple = Row

Attribute = Column

---

## Mistake 2

Confusing Degree and Cardinality.

Degree = Columns

Cardinality = Rows

---

## Mistake 3

Thinking Relation means Relationship.

Relation = Table

Relationship = Association between entities.

---

## Mistake 4

Ignoring Domain Constraints.

Domains ensure valid data entry.
