# 💬 FAQs

### What is a Distributed Database?

A database distributed across multiple physical locations but managed as one system.

---

### Why use Replication?

Improve availability and fault tolerance.

---

### What is Fragmentation?

Dividing data into smaller pieces across nodes.

---

### What is 2PC?

A protocol that coordinates distributed transaction commits.

---

### What is 3PC?

An improved commit protocol with an additional pre-commit phase.

---

### What is CAP Theorem?

A distributed system can guarantee only two of Consistency, Availability, and Partition Tolerance at the same time.
