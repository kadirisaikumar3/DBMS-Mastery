# 🎯 Google Interview Corner

## Most Asked Concepts

### Fragmentation

Splits database into smaller pieces.

Types:

- Horizontal
- Vertical
- Hybrid

---

### Replication

Stores copies of data across multiple locations.

Benefits:

- High Availability
- Fault Tolerance
- Faster Reads

---

### Two-Phase Commit (2PC)

Coordinator

↓

Prepare Phase

↓

Commit Phase

Ensures all participating databases commit together.

---

### Three-Phase Commit (3PC)

Improves 2PC by reducing blocking.

Adds:

Pre-Commit Phase

---

### CAP Theorem

Consistency

Availability

Partition Tolerance

Distributed systems can fully guarantee only two simultaneously.

---
