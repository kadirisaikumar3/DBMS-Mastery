# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a respectful and welcoming experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of positive behavior include:

- Being respectful
- Helping beginners
- Providing constructive feedback
- Encouraging learning
- Being patient with others

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment
- Personal attacks
- Hate speech
- Spam
- Offensive comments

## Enforcement

Project maintainers have the responsibility to remove inappropriate contributions.

Thank you for helping make this repository welcoming to everyone.
