# ❌ Common Mistakes

## Mistake 1

Creating Indexes on every column.

Too many indexes reduce write performance.

---

## Mistake 2

Confusing Clustered and Non-Clustered Indexes.

Clustered:

Stores Data Order

Non-Clustered:

Stores Pointers

---

## Mistake 3

Ignoring Composite Indexes.

Useful for multi-column searches.

---

## Mistake 4

Thinking Indexes only improve performance.

Indexes improve reads but may slow writes.
