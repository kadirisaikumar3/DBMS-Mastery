# ❌ Common Mistakes

## Mistake 1

Thinking Views store data.

Views do not store actual data.

---

## Mistake 2

Using too many nested Views.

Can reduce performance.

---

## Mistake 3

Confusing Trigger and Stored Procedure.

Triggers execute automatically.

Stored Procedures execute manually.

---

## Mistake 4

Creating unnecessary Triggers.

May impact performance.
