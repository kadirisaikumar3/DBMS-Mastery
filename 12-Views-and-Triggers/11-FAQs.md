# 💬 FAQs

### What is a View?

A virtual table created from a query.

---

### Does a View store data?

No.

---

### What is a Trigger?

Automatic database action.

---

### Which events activate Triggers?

INSERT

UPDATE

DELETE

---

### Why use Views?

Security and Abstraction.

---

### Why use Triggers?

Automation and Data Integrity.
