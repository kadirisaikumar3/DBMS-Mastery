# ❌ Common Mistakes

## Mistake 1

Confusing Procedure and Function.

Procedure:

Can perform actions.

Function:

Returns value.

---

## Mistake 2

Writing business logic repeatedly.

Use Stored Procedures for reuse.

---

## Mistake 3

Ignoring Parameters.

Input and Output parameters improve flexibility.

---

## Mistake 4

Creating overly large Procedures.

Large Procedures become difficult to maintain.

---

## Mistake 5

Hardcoding values inside Procedures.

Prefer parameters.
